Italian Chicken Pasta

Description

This pasta dish is a simple and stress-free recipe that anyone can prepare. Using only a handful of ingredients and a crockpot, this Italian Chicken Pasta has an addictive flavor that goes well with breadsticks, rolls, or simply by itself.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Chicken breasts
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 16 oz Italian Dressing
  • 16 oz Pasta
  • 1/2 Cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Place your thawed chicken breast at the bottom of your crockpot.
  2. Place the cream cheese, italian dressing, and grated cheese atop the chicken.
  3. Cook on low for 6 hours.
  4. Cook your pasta and shred the now-cooked chicken.
  5. Mix your pasta into the crockpot and enjoy.
