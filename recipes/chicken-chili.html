Chicken Chili

Description

This chicken chili recipe requires little hassle and cooks up quickly in a crockpot. With a dash of chili powder, this chicken chili is sure to warm you up in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz Black Beans
  • 15 oz Corn
  • 10 oz Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Packet of Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 2 Chicken Breasts

Steps

  1. Place your thawed chicken breast at the bottom of your crockpot.
  2. Place the black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, cream cheese, and spices / mixes atop the chicken.
  3. Cook on low for 8 hours.
  4. Shred the cooked chicken and enjoy.
