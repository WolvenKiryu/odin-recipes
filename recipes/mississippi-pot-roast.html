Mississippi Pot Roast

Description

A simple pot roast bursting with flavor and a hint of spice.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chuck Roast
  • 1 Packet of Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1 Packet of Au Jus Gravy Mix
  • 6-7 Pepperoncini Peppers
  • 1 Stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Place your chuck roast at the bottom of your crockpot.
  2. Sprinkle your two mixes atop the chuck roast.
  3. Place your peppers and butter on top of your now-seasoned roast.
  4. Cook on low for 8 hours.
  5. Shred the roast and enjoy.
